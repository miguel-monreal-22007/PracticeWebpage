Red Dead Redemption

Arthur Morgan

Arthur Morgan is the main character, and the Van Der Linde gangs main fighter. Before contracting tuberculosis who was very rough, and focused on getting things done. Though as the story progressed he aimed to be a better person who can help others as he couldn't help himself.

"You sir are a fish."

Dutch Van Der Linde

Dutch Van Der Linde is the leader of the Van Der Linde gang who has raised many of the members in it since they were children. After Micah joined the gang Dutch started to become a lot more ruthless wanting to cause chaos wherever he went. He's very cunning and cares mostly about money and loyalty.

"I have a plan."

Sadie Adler

Sadie Adler is a widow who joined the gang after her husband was killed by the O'driscoll gang. As a woman in 1899 she had been restricted or not expected to do any fighting though she always asked to. After Dutch and the main fighters got stranded on Guarma she was able to keep the gang together and save them from danger. After this her and Arthur became very close friends, she found him to be the only person she could trust. By the end she became one of the main fighters in the gang.

"I ain't afraid of dying."

John Marston

John Marston is the secondary main character, and a main fighter for the Van Der Linde gang. Arthur was always close to John and wanted to give him a good life. After the events in 1899 John decided to settle down as rancher with his wife Abigail and his son Jack. Though settling down is still difficult due to the life he has lived.

"Revenge is a fool's game."

Micah Bell

Micah Bell joined the gang recently and as soon as he joined things started to go downhill. Most robberies would end in the law being alerted immediately and Dutch began to change for the worse. Arthur and many of the better people in the gang had never fully trusted Micah with him being quite a nuisance. In the end he turned out to be a rat all along.

"I'm a survivor, Black Lung!"
